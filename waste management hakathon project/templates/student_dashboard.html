Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my complaint?
You can track your complaint by visiting the "My Complaints" section. Each complaint has a unique tracking ID that you can use to check its status. You'll also receive email updates when the status changes.
What is the expected resolution time?
Resolution time depends on the priority level: High priority issues are typically resolved within 24 hours, Medium within 48 hours, and Low within 72 hours. Complex issues may take longer.
Can I submit anonymous complaints?
Yes, you can submit complaints anonymously. However, providing your contact information helps us follow up if we need more details about the issue.
What types of issues can I report?
You can report any waste management-related issues including overflowing bins, improper waste segregation, hazardous waste, blocked drains, and general cleanliness concerns.
How do I add images to my complaint?
You can now add images to your complaint! Simply click on the upload area in the complaint form, select up to 5 images, and they will be attached to your complaint. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, and GIF (max 5MB each).
